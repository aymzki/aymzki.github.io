About Me

My name is Alissa and I am from California, currently living in the Philadelphia area. Previously I was a medical student, but with clinical rotations I did not see myself in the field and wanted to figure out a different life. I’m excited to enter this field—there are so many possibilities! My favourite pastimes are cooking and baking but I took that a step further and have my current job as a line cook/pastry chef. I also love dogs, and my favorite music include Electroswing and Bollywood.
