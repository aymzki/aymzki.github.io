About Me

I am a UX Designer, former medical student, chef, and coder. These roles have taught me to solve problems with empathy, curiosity, and creativity, working with diverse groups of people. I have lived in Japan, Europe, and the United States, and remotely work with a global team, seeing different perspectives, thriving in different environments, and bridging different thoughts and cultures. I aim to improve everyday digital experiences and communicate transparency and trust through my designs.
