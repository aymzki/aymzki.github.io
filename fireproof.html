Why I made this project

113 home fires were reported in Philadelphia in 2023, showing the urgent need for developing an app for fire safety. By creating an app that provides important information on fire prevention, safety measures, and emergency response, we can help raise awareness and empower individuals to take proactive steps to protect themselves and their homes.

Market Research

Although the incidences of residential fires decreased nationwide in the past decade, overall deaths in residential fires have increased by 8%, reaching a high of 2,840 deaths in 2021. Many of these deaths may not have happened had there been increased prevention and awareness.
